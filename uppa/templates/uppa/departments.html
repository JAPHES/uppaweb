Our Departments

UPPA departments work together as one system. Each unit contributes specific expertise to ensure projects are designed well, funded responsibly, and delivered with measurable impact.

Department Structure

Each department plays a unique role in delivering quality projects and strengthening youth-centered development programs.

Project Management Department

Leads planning, coordination, execution, and evaluation of UPPA projects across different regions.

Finance and Funding Department

Oversees budgeting, resource allocation, financial reporting, and sustainable funding partnerships.

ICT Department

Drives digital operations, tools, and systems that improve collaboration and program effectiveness.

Research, Innovation and Development

Builds evidence-based solutions and pilots ideas that respond to local challenges and opportunities.

Communications Department

Manages internal communication, public visibility, and consistent messaging across all channels.

Social Media Department

Expands community engagement through storytelling, campaigns, and platform-driven communication.
