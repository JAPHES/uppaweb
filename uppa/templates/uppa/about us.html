What is UPPA?

UPPA (Universal Project Partnership Association) is a community-focused organization that brings together individuals from diverse backgrounds to collaborate on projects promoting youth empowerment, skills development, leadership, and entrepreneurship. Its mission is to create sustainable opportunities, solve community challenges, and support personal and collective growth through training, partnerships, and innovation.
